About Lord Ganesha

  • Ganesha, also spelled Ganesh, also called Ganapati, elephant-headed Hindu god of beginnings.
  • Ganesha is known as the remover of obstacles.
  • The Elephant god teaches us to keep an open mind, think prudently, and seek knowledge.
  • Dinka the mouse, vehicle of Ganesha, represents speed and sharpness.
